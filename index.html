How to get Roblox Executer

  1. Go to this link and click on Allow.
  2. Go to Settings > General > VPN & Device Management. You should see koindns. Click it, then click Install and enter your password (if you have one). Click Install again.
  3. Stay in VPN & Device Management and click on DNS. Make sure Adware Filter 1 and Adware Filter 2 are turned on.
  4. Go to your home screen. You should see an app called esign khoindvn. Open it.
  5. Scroll down until you see esign bypass revoke. Click avex digital INC or any of the esigns (this is the name of your cert).
  6. Open the esign. If the message shows the name of your cert, go to Settings > VPN & Device Management and click your cert's name to verify it (you might need to restart your device; always click Trust).
  7. After trusting the cert, open the app again and agree to everything.
  8. Go back to the esign khoindvn app and click Download esign certificate. Click More and save it to your files.
  9. In the esign app, click the three dots at the top, then click Import. Find the esign certs, click it, then click Unzip.
  10. After unzipping, you should see a folder called esign certs. Find the name of your cert, click it, and select Import Certificate Management. It should say Imported Successfully. Click the back arrow.
  11. Go to this link. If you're in Safari, click the download and click the icon next to the refresh button (the share icon). Instead of sending the file, click Save to Files. If you see a More option, click it and then Save to Files.
  12. Open the esign app, click the three dots, and click Import. Find Delta, click it, then click Unzip. If a message like Import to App Library pops up, click No.
  13. After unzipping, you should see a folder called Payload. Click it, then click roblox.app. Click Signature and make sure Install After Signed is turned on. Click Signature and wait.
  14. It should pop up with a message like esign.yyyue.xyz would like to install Delta. Click Install. If it pops up multiple times, click Cancel. Boom, it's installed!

Note: If you need help with getting the Delta key, search on YouTube or elsewhere.
